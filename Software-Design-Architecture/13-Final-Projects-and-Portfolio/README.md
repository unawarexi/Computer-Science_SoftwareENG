# 13 - Final Projects and Portfolio

Apply everything you've learned by designing and building a full system from scratch. Showcase your skills and prepare for real-world architect roles.

## What You'll Learn

- End-to-end system design
- Architecture documentation and diagrams
- CI/CD and deployment
- Portfolio and presentation skills

## Weekly Breakdown

**Week 1–2: Design a Full System**
- Requirements gathering, system design, APIs, architecture diagrams, CI/CD, deployment

**Week 3–4: Share & Review**
- Publish on GitHub, write a blog post, get feedback

---

By the end, you'll have a portfolio-ready project to showcase your architecture skills.
